Diploma in housekeeping

Manage duties and chores of housekeeping

Housekeeping is a service that revolves around maintaining the structure, cleanliness and comfort of varied establishments and organizations for which individuals work. To the fast growing modern world, housekeeping sector offers its importance in providing the serene way of ambient and is becoming a keen sector within the facility management circle which has its significance throughout. After completion of this specific course, candidates may get opportunities in areas like Hotels, Resorts, Tour and Travel Agencies, Restaurants, Lodges, Guesthouses, etc. Candidates can also work in job positions like Laundryman, Linen Keeper, Tailor and Laundry Master.

Syllabus.

  • Housekeeping operations
  • General hotel maintenance
  • Hygiene and sanitation
  • Basic business communication
